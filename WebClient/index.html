How can we Help?

How to use our application?

With our navigation system on top of our website you can view different section of our application, the navigation bar is a user interface that intends to provide customers aid in accessing information in different pages (Home, Product, About, Help, Login).

How to search for products?

In our Product page we provide users with two level of searches. The first level is Key board search using our search box, users can input words or letters and our application will automatically look for matching products that contains same name or holds one or more similar letters specified by user input. The second level of search is browsing, using our tab bar users can now look through different categories that of our product & services provides from ALL products through Accessories, Keyboards & mouse, headsets, gaming hardware, mobile phones, and software.

How to see my Order?

If your a user with an account in our system you can see your order within the Cart page there you should be able to see how many items you've added to your cart and the total amount due if you decided to purchase each product. Within the Cart page you're also able to remove or increase and decrease the quantity of products you want ot buy.

How to create my account?

In our Login page if your not registered in our system yet, click on 'Register an Account' then after user authentication is granted you can now modify your own detail and submit an order for the company products and services.
